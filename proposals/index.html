Overview

  1. Reward & Recognition - Healthcare Staff

3 / 8

Objective

  • Improve motivation and performance at work

  • Provide a perfect platform for ongoing recognition

  • Employees receive points for going above and beyond

  • After accumulating 50 points, employees can redeem their points for gifts
4 / 8

Awardees

All Healthcare Staff in Rest Home and Hospital:
  • Registered nurses

  • Enrolled nurses

  • Healthcare assistants
5 / 8

1) Attendance Award (10 points)

  • On the first week of every third month, each staff member is evaluated for their attendance in the previous 3 months.

  • All staff who have clocked in and out on time on every allocated shift receive the attendance award.

  • Staff are not disqualified for days taken off due to sick leave etc.

  • Budget: estimated that 10-30 staff will receive this award per 3 months ($400 - $1200 per year)
6 / 8

2) Resident's Choice Award (10 points)

  • On the first week of every month, 10 residents from each of the resthome and hospital will be randomly surveyed and asked to recommend 1 or more staff member who has been caring and helpful to them in the past month.

  • The person who has the most votes receives this award. If there is a tie, the awardee is selected randomly among the tied.

  • One monthly award for resthome and one for hospital.

  • The resident’s votes remain anonymous.

  • Budget: 24 awards per year (maximum of $240 per year)
7 / 8

3) Spotlight Award (20 points)

  • Awarded to someone who demonstrates leadership and/or critical thinking during a difficult/exceptional situation. The awardee is an inspiration who went above and beyond.

  • The Lifecare Manager and Clinical Manager may award this to any Healthcare Staff at any time.

  • Each RN is allocated 2 badges of the Spotlight Award per 6 month period which they may award to any HCA's at their own discretion

  • Budget: up to 40 badges per year (maximum $800 per year)
8 / 8

Reward

After accruing 50 points, a staff member may redeem their points for one of the following:
  1. $50 Pak'n Save voucher
  2. $50 Petrol voucher
  3. $50 Westfield voucher
  4. 60 minute Massage voucher
  5. $50 Beauty care voucher
  6. Dinner for two
  7. $50 AT hop top up voucher
The rewards are purchased in advance and are redeemed subject to availability.
